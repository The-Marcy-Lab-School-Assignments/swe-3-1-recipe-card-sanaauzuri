Fried Sweet Plantains

Home Ingredients Instructions

Instructions

  1. Cut off the ends of your plantains, slit a side of the plantain, and peel off the skin.
  2. Slice the plantains diagonally, as thick or thin as you prefer.
  3. Heat a quarter inch of oil into your pan, over medium heat.
  4. When the oil shimmers, add the plantain slices beside each other. Fry each side 2-3 minutes, until golden brown.
  5. Scoop out the plantains with a fork or slotted spoon, and place them onto a paper towel or onto the skin you initially peeled off the plantain to drain the excess oil.
  6. Optionally, sprinkle with salt or your choice of garnish.
