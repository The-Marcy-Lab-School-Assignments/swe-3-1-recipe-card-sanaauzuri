Fried Sweet Plantains

Instructions

  1. For the sweetest fried plantains, use very ripe, or blackened plantains. Cut off the ends of your plantains, make a slit down the side, and peel off the skin.
  2. Slice the plantain diagonally, as thick or thin as you prefer.
  3. Pour a quarter inch of oil into your pan over medium heat until the oil shimmers.
  4. Lay the plantain slices next to eachother, do not crowd the pan or overlap the plantains. Fry for 2-3 minutes per side, until golden brown.
  5. Remove the plantain from the pan using a fork or spoon, and place them on a paper towel to drain the excess oil.
  6. Optional: Sprinkle with salt or your choice of garnish.
